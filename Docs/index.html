About Me

Growing up in Austin and having been born into a family of musicians it was natural for me to spend thirty years on the road, on stage, and in music schools. Along the way, a natural love of languages and logic led to programming and coding. The design of engaging and interactive sites and applications is what occupies my energies today.

Seems hard to believe today, but I first laid my hands on an Apple computer in central Austin in 1980.

In a marketplace flooded with developers, my unique offering is a highly evolved and varied aesthetic sense, tinged with humor and warmth.
